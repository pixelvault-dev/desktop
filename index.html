Copy an image — paste a hosted URL.

Two ways to grab an image — both give you a hosted URL to paste:

  • Press ⇧⌘2 to capture a screen region.
  • Or copy any image (⇧⌃⌘4 copies a region to the clipboard).

The "Image URL copied" notification is your cue — then ⌘V to paste it into your coding agent.
